School Village connects experts with passionate learners.
Innovation and impact are our guiding principles.

  • History

    School Village was founded in 2009, as a local tutoring service in South Florida. After serving thousands of students, we expanded our focus to facilitating connections between educators and learners around the globe. Our team, with over 10 years of experience in education, has expanded to tackle global learning and build great technology.

  • Approach

    School Village connects experts (tutors, trainers, etc.) with anyone who want to learn. We achieve this by leveraging the newest technologies to find the smartest people in each locale.   • Culture

    We are a team of tinkerers, builders, and educators on a mission. We build solutions quickly and quantitatively so that experts and learners can enjoy them. Our team includes several musicians. When we're not working on spreadsheets and APIs, we have jam sessions and eat Caribbean food.
